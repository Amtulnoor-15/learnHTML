John Wick

John Wick is a legendary character portrayed by Keanu Reeves in a groundbreaking action-thriller series. A feared assassin pulled back into the criminal world by fate, his story is one of grief, revenge, and relentless determination - all while looking sharp in a suit.

Biography

John Wick once walked away from a world of violence to be with the woman he loved. But after her death, and a senseless act of cruelty, the killing of a puppy she left him, John returns with a fury. What follows is a high-octane storm of bullets, broken bones, and stylish vengeance.

Key Characteristics

  • Elite hand-to-hand combat and firearm expert
  • Unstoppable focus and calm under pressure
  • Operates with a strict code of honor
  • Feared in the underworld as "Baba Yaga"
