John Wick

John Wick is a fictional character portrayed by Keanu Reeves in the popular action thriller film series. Known as a legendary assassin with a strict code of honor, John Wick’s story revolves around his quest for vengeance and survival in a dangerous underworld.

Biography

John Wick was a feared hitman who retired to live a peaceful life with his wife. After her tragic death and a cruel attack on him involving the killing of his puppy—a last gift from his wife—Wick returns to his deadly skills to seek justice.

Key Characteristics

  • Master of hand-to-hand combat and marksmanship
  • Relentless, focused, and resourceful
  • Code of honor despite being an assassin
  • Known as "Baba Yaga" in the assassin world

Ballerina (Spin-off)

Ballerina is a spin-off movie set in the John Wick universe. Released in 2024, it follows a young female assassin seeking revenge while navigating the dangerous criminal underworld.
